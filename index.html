2025 Ride Engineering Competition

Chrono Clash was CU Boulder Theme Park Engineering and Design Club's (TPED) first entry into the Ride Engineering Competition. Working with 10 interdisciplinary students we took a functional, motor-powered, flat ride for Starburst riders from ideation to fabrication. Teams were scored on various criteria including ASTM F2291-24 standard compliance, Factory and Site Acceptance Testing and ride uptime during the 6-hour run period.
