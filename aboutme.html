About Me

Hello, I'm Jeremy Sirof, a third year mechanical engineering student at CU Boulder. I remember the magic of walking into Diagon Alley for the first time, feeling a mountain Banshee from Avatar breathing beneath me transporting me into the fictional worlds. My time with CU Boulder Theme Park Engineering Design Club has fostered my love for themed entertainment and looking at it from an engineer's perspective. Working on Chrono Clash and our current project, creating an escape room, has affirmed I want to engineer these experiences that bring awe and joy to others.

Outside of engineering I love spending my time rock climbing, where I compete for the CU Boulder Climbing Team, or hiking up in the mountains.
