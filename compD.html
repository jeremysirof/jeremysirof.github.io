Drill Powered Vehicle

September 2025 â€“ December 2025

Component Design: Design and build a Drill Powered Vehicle capable of climbing a hill with added weight.

As CAD Engineer, I brought our bike from initial sketches to finalized CAD model and drawing package in SolidWorks. I utilized weldments when designing the frame to ensure dimensional accuracy and manufacturability. I also created subassemblies for our drill plate and front fork to confirm our mechanical systems would be functional. I developed a drawing package for all parts to be manufactured through various processes including coping, cutting, welding, milling, and use of waterjet. Collaborating with in house, professional manufacturers, I revised the drawings until they were up to professional standard. Our vehicle was able travel on flat terrain with over 1000lbs and to ascend the hill with a total weight of 300lbs.
